Curriculum Vitae

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at HCP Lab, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun-Yat-Sen University, working with Prof. Liang Lin.
I obtained my Ph.D degree of Telecommunications and Information Systems in 2019 at Phoenix-eye Lab, School of Telecommunications Engineering,
Xidian University, advised by Prof. Zhaoyang Lu. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in telecommunications engineering, School of Information Engineering,
Chang’an University in 2014. I am the reviewer of IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TMM, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE THMS, IEEE SPL, PR, and NSFC. I am the IEEE Member.
My research interests include:

  • Computer Vision: Video Analysis and Understanding, Human Action Recognition
  • Machine Learning: Transfer Learning, Domain Adaptation, Knowledge Distillation
  • Representation Learning: Deep Learning, Self-supervised Learning, Reinforcement Learning
  • Intelligent Transportation System: Anomaly Detection, Traffic Prediction, Data Mining
